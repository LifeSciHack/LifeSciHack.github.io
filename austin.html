About This Event

Bridging the Gap between Life Science and Software

LifeSci Hack is Austin’s first-ever bio-hackathon and we’re focusing on 4 core pain-points felt by those in the life science community:

1. Interoperability

Tracking of information between software.

2. Big Data

Dealing with large scientific data data for implementation and analysis.

3. Life Science Tools

Software package tools for the life science community.

4. BioEducation

More robust educational tools for the life science community.

We want to highlight the benefits of collaborating together across experiences and industries from patient care to biotechnology research. We plan to build active, helpful resources over 3 days at WeWork Austin.

Anyone who has a skill is invited from graphic design to programming to writing. Join us and we’ll feed you while you hack. We’ll have prizes too including SXSW badges and more.

Why Attend?

Bridging the Gap between Life Science and Software

Be Inspired

We are now at the pinnacle of creativity with new emerging technologies and innovative practices like design thinking and hacking. Join others to build your idea and knowledge.

Meet New Faces

We are the first event of this kind in Austin. With our company, academic, and technology invite list, you’ll be sure to meet some amazing people (and possible new friends.)

Get Hired

Can’t seem to find an employer or employee? Our invite list includes recruiters to help you all find who you are looking for and great fits based on the results of our hackathon.

Free Swag

There will be fresh food, cash and SXSW badge prizes up for grabs, stickers and much more.

Global-Facing

Our event is the start of something much larger and we can’t wait to showcase our hackers and projects in front of a global audience. You never know who might be listening or watching!

Learn Something New

Regardless of your experience, who is in your current network or what your skills might be, there is always an opportunity to learn at an event like this. Time to level up in the new year!

What to Bring?

Bridging the Gap between Life Science and Software

Pre-liminary to the event we will have a meetup for future hackers to showcase or talk more about their ideas and spur innovation. You are allowed to bring existing code to work on before the event begins so long as the project is not at it's near completion. Feel free to also use any recycled projects!

Check List

  • Bring a positive, can-do attitude
  • Your laptop (we won’t have spaces for a desktop setup)
  • Your own food if you have certain dietary requirements
  • Warm and comfortable clothes
  • A water bottle you can refill to stay hydrated
  • Your resume
  • Your existing code or recyclable material, everything is open source
